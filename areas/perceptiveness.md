# Perception

- how does one become perceptive?
- what does it mean to be perceptive?


In order to be perceptive you need:
- agency
- introspection


Agency is being effective in the world, this means effectively achieving goals
by tackling problems presented by reality?

Introspection is the act of reflecting upon oneself. It is knowing about and
understanding your emotions clearly.

Introspection-by-doing combines agency & introspection
